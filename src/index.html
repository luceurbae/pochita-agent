Risk Management

Protection built into the loop

Pochita doesn't trade on impulse — every decision passes through hard filters first.

—
RSI Hard Boundaries
Skips if RSI is 45–55 (neutral) or above 70 (overbought/oversold).
—
Trend Conflict Filter
Skips when 15m and 5m trends conflict — avoiding sideways noise.
—
Buy Pressure Threshold
UP signal requires 55%+ buy pressure. DOWN requires 45% or less.
—
Price Delta Gate
Skips if |price delta| is below $5 — no entries on small movements.
—
Conviction Floor
Minimum 80% AI conviction required. Below threshold means skip.
—
AI Bias Alignment
AI prediction must match rule engine bias. If not aligned, it is skipped.
