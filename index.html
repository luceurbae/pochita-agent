What's Pochita?

Pochita is an autonomous trading bot specialized in Bitcoin 15-minute markets on Polymarket. It analyzes price action, trend, momentum (RSI), support/resistance levels, and fair value gaps (FVG) to make decisions.

How it Works

Every 15 minutes, Pochita fetches price data from the active market slot. It calculates conviction based on multiple factors — price position vs. PTB, trend direction, RSI conditions, and chart patterns. Entry only triggers when conviction ≥80% and price ≤$0.50. Takes profit at 2×. Maximum loss per trade: $5.
